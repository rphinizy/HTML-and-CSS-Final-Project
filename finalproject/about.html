Hello! My name is Robin and I learned how to do balloon twisting and sculpting during the Covid-19 lockdown.
My plan was to make some simple animals for the kids for the annual summer party at my full time job.


The lockdown continued and the annual party was canceled. I started to make balloon sculpture and arrangements
for families to take home and celebrate birthdays and other holidays.I started to get really good at it... and really busy!


If you would like to see more of my work, please visit the gallery page or follow me on instagram!
